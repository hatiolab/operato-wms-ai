  - GET `/rest/oms_dashboard/biz_type_stats`
  - GET `/rest/oms_dashboard/channel_stats`
  - GET `/rest/oms_dashboard/wave_stats`
  - GET `/rest/oms_dashboard/allocation_stats`
  - GET `/rest/oms_dashboard/cutoff_alerts`
